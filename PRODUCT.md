# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Herwanto is the primary user, usually checking the assistant from a private mobile PWA or Telegram flow while planning, triaging, drafting, marking, or reviewing the day.

## Product Purpose

H.I.R.A is a personal AI operating surface for schedule awareness, reminders, project tracking, briefings, files, Gmail triage, and assistant chat. Success means the next useful action is obvious, the current load feels legible, and the interface reduces mental friction.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, quietly technical. The product should feel like a reliable assistant console: focused, restrained, and direct without becoming sterile.

## Anti-references

Avoid mixed novelty fonts, competing display sizes, decorative typography in controls, generic dashboard clutter, marketing-page drama, and visual noise that makes the user work to understand the hierarchy.

## Design Principles

- Keep the task visible before the aesthetic.
- Use hierarchy to lower cognitive load, not to decorate.
- Preserve the H.I.R.A console identity through structure, rhythm, and restraint.
- Prefer consistent product patterns over one-off visual inventions.
- Make mobile reading and repeated use comfortable first.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, predictable focus states, readable body text, reduced-motion compatibility, and status cues that do not depend on color alone.
