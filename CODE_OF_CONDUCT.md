# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be respectful, specific, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be considerate of different experience levels and contexts.
- Keep feedback actionable and focused on the work.
- Assume private user data is sensitive by default.
- Disagree directly without personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private information, credentials, logs, student/user data, or personal
  documents without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests after maintainers have asked to
  return to the topic.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; reject pull
requests; or block participants when needed to keep the project safe and
productive.
